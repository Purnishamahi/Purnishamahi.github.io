India's Flower Festivals

Bathukamma Festival - Telangana

Celebrated by the women of Telangana, Bathukamma is a vibrant floral festival that marks the beauty of nature. Women create flower stacks arranged in concentric layers and dance around them with devotional songs. It’s not just a celebration of flowers but also a tribute to womanhood and local culture.

Ooty Flower Show - Tamil Nadu

Held annually in May at the Government Botanical Gardens, the Ooty Flower Show displays over 150 flower varieties, including rare orchids and exotic hybrids. The floral sculptures and intricate arrangements attract thousands of nature lovers from all over the country.

Rose Festival - Chandigarh

This grand event at Zakir Hussain Rose Garden features more than 1600 varieties of roses. Along with the floral display, the festival includes cultural performances, competitions, and food fairs, drawing more than 300,000 visitors every year.

Onam Pookalam - Kerala

During the harvest festival of Onam, people across Kerala design intricate flower carpets called 'Pookalam' in front of their homes. Each design is a colorful tribute to prosperity, unity, and devotion. It's a highlight of the ten-day-long cultural celebrations of the state.
