Colombia's Flower Festivals

Feria de las Flores – Medellín

This world-renowned event takes place every August in Medellín, celebrating Colombia’s rich floral heritage. The highlight is the Desfile de Silleteros, where flower farmers carry elaborate floral arrangements on their backs in a parade. The festival also features concerts, flower exhibitions, and antique car parades.

ExpoFlor – Bogotá

Held in the capital, ExpoFlor is a vibrant trade fair showcasing Colombia's export-quality flowers. It brings together florists, growers, and enthusiasts to witness cutting-edge designs, new flower varieties, and sustainable growing practices. Educational workshops and eco-friendly flower art displays are major draws.

Festival de la Florística – Boyacá

This lesser-known but beautiful festival held in Boyacá focuses on the natural biodiversity and native flowers of the Andean region. The event emphasizes ecological education, native flower preservation, and rural community participation with floral-themed dances and storytelling sessions.

Festival de Flores de Cali

Cali’s floral celebration fuses tropical blooms with vibrant music and dance. Held in spring, the festival includes parades of floats decorated with flowers, live salsa performances, and street flower markets. It highlights both Colombia’s love of flowers and its rich Afro-Latin cultural roots.
