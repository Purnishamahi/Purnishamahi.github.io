Flower Care & Tutorials

Peony

  • Plant in full sun with well-drained soil.
  • Water deeply but infrequently.
  • Support large blooms with cages or stakes.
  • Deadhead to encourage more blooms.

Rose

  • Provide 6–8 hours of sunlight daily.
  • Prune annually to remove dead wood.
  • Use mulch to retain moisture.
  • Fertilize monthly during growing season.

Sunflower

  • Grow in full sun and direct sow after frost.
  • Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • Stake tall varieties to prevent toppling.
  • Harvest seeds once heads turn brown.

Orchid

  • Use orchid-specific potting mix.
  • Water once a week, allow drainage.
  • Place in indirect sunlight.
  • Fertilize with orchid food monthly.

Lily

  • Plant bulbs in spring or fall.
  • Full sun to partial shade is ideal.
  • Water moderately; avoid overwatering.
  • Remove faded flowers to boost bloom.

Tulip

  • Plant bulbs in autumn in well-drained soil.
  • Water after planting; avoid waterlogging.
  • Allow foliage to die back naturally.
  • Store bulbs cool and dry for replanting.
