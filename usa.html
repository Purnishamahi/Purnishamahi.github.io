USA Flower Festivals

National Cherry Blossom Festival – Washington, D.C.

Celebrated each spring, this iconic festival honors the 1912 gift of cherry trees from Tokyo. The Tidal Basin is transformed with soft pink blossoms, accompanied by parades, art exhibits, performances, and fireworks along the Potomac River.

The Flower Fields – Carlsbad, California

From March to May, fifty acres of Giant Tecolote Ranunculus bloom in a vibrant rainbow across the rolling hills of Carlsbad. Visitors enjoy tractor rides, themed gardens, and panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean in the distance.

Skagit Valley Tulip Festival – Washington State

Every April, the fertile Skagit Valley becomes a sea of color with rows upon rows of tulips. This month-long celebration includes tulip-themed art, family-friendly events, photo contests, and farm tours in a stunning countryside setting.

Portland Rose Festival – Oregon

Dating back to 1907, the Portland Rose Festival features the Grand Floral Parade, dragon boat races, and the crowning of the Rose Queen. The city’s nickname, “City of Roses,” comes to life with thousands of blooms showcased in elaborate displays.
