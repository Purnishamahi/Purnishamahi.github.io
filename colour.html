Flower Symbolism by Color

Red - The Color of Passion

Bold and captivating, red flowers embody deep love, desire, and strength. A timeless expression of fiery devotion.

Rose • Tulip • Poppy

Yellow - Sunshine in Bloom

Radiating warmth and joy, yellow blooms signify friendship, happiness, and new beginnings.

Sunflower • Daisy • Marigold

Pink - Whispers of Grace

A hue of admiration and joy, pink flowers express gentle love, grace, and playfulness.

Peony • Rose • Carnation

White - The Purest Petals

White flowers symbolize purity, innocence, and eternal devotion. Often found in weddings and remembrance.

Lily • Orchid • Daisy

Purple - The Royal Bloom

Mysterious and elegant, purple flowers represent enchantment, royalty, and deep admiration.

Orchid • Lavender • Violet

Orange - A Burst of Energy

Vibrant and uplifting, orange flowers symbolize enthusiasm, warmth, and encouragement.

Marigold • Lily • Gerbera

Blue - The Tranquil Petal

Rare and soothing, blue flowers evoke peace, trust, and calmness, like a gentle sky.

Hydrangea • Bluebell • z Delphinium
